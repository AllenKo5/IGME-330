Yu-Gi-Oh! Card Finder

Yu-Gi-Oh! is a Japanese collectible card game developed and published by Konami. It is based on the fictional game of Duel Monsters created by manga artist Kazuki Takahashi, which appears in portions of the manga franchise Yu-Gi-Oh!. This project aims to help with easy searching and filtering of the thousands of cards currently in the game.
